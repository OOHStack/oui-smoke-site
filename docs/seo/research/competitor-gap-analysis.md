## Topics Oui Smoke can cover better

1. **Operations transparency** — live platform (ops console, client portal, guest QR) is a distinctive, demonstrable differentiator most competitors lack.
2. **Clear package math** — public GTA rate table + estimator (already strong).
3. **Premium brand craft** — visual identity already exceeds typical local SEO templates; keep it.
4. **Partner pathway** — `/partner` one-pager for planners/venues.
5. **Honest adult-service framing** — age requirements and no false health claims.

## Weak or generic competitor patterns (strategic note)

Many local pages rely on thin city swaps, superlatives (“best”, “#1”), and sparse logistics detail. Oui Smoke should **not** mirror that. Prefer operational specificity, venue planning guidance, and firsthand event process.

## Missing intent coverage (pre-implementation)

- Event intent: wedding, corporate, private, festival/hospitality
- Location intent: consolidated GTA hub first; city pages only with unique content
- Informational: costs, guest counts, venue requirements, outdoor planning
- Comparison: staffed catering vs DIY rental

## Trust / logistics gaps vs competitors

Oui Smoke baseline lacked: About page, testimonials block, published phone (may be intentional), response-time expectations, cancellation summary on marketing pages, insurance language.

**Do not invent these.** Collect via owner checklist.

## Quotability opportunities for search + LLMs

Publish short, factual answer blocks for:

- What Oui Smoke is
- Where it serves
- Staffed vs rental
- What hosts must confirm with venues
- Age requirement
- How booking works (4 steps)

Use consistent NAP/entity strings across homepage, About, schema, and `llms.txt`.

## Research limitations

- Fetches respect robots.txt, rate limits, and allowlists; some sites may block or return sparse HTML.
- No private social scraping.
- No reuse of competitor wording or assets.
- Manual import file: `docs/seo/research/manual-urls.txt`.
- Inventory CSV is regenerated by the research script when network fetch is run.
