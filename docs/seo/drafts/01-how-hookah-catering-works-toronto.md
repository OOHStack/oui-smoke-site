## Direct answer

Mobile hookah catering brings a staffed shisha experience to your venue or private property. For Oui Smoke events in Toronto and the GTA, the process typically runs: inquiry → package confirmation → logistics alignment → delivery/setup → managed service → cleanup.

## Who this is for

Hosts planning private celebrations, weddings, or corporate hospitality who want a managed experience rather than DIY equipment.

## Step-by-step

1. **Inquiry** — Share date, city/venue, guest count, indoor/outdoor plan, preferred unit count and hours via `/book`.  
2. **Confirmation** — Availability and package fit are confirmed; deposit timing follows current payment settings.  
3. **Logistics** — Align load-in, setup window, service duration, and add-ons. Venue permission is the client’s responsibility.  
4. **Event day** — Delivery, setup, staffing, guest service support, and cleanup.  

## Venue and legal notes

- Confirm building/venue rules before booking.  
- Outdoor plans need weather and space consideration.  
- Adult-oriented service; Ontario legal age requirements apply (19+).  
- This guide is operational advice, not legal advice.
